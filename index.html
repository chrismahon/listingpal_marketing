Frequently asked questions

What types of photos work best?

ListingPal works with any standard property photos — interior shots, exterior views, and garden/outdoor spaces. JPG, PNG, and HEIC formats are all supported. Better lighting leads to richer descriptions, but the AI handles a wide range of photo quality.

Can I edit the generated description?

Absolutely. Every generated listing is fully editable before you copy or export it. You can also regenerate specific sections, adjust the tone, or add custom details like nearby schools, transport links, or local amenities.

How does the branded PDF work?

Upload your agency logo and set your brand colours in your account settings once. From then on, every PDF export automatically uses your branding, presenting the listing with your contact details in a professional, print-ready layout.

Is my data and my clients' data secure?

Yes. Photos are processed securely and are never used to train our AI models. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are fully GDPR compliant and never share your data with third parties.

Can I use ListingPal for commercial properties?

Yes. ListingPal works for residential and commercial properties alike — offices, retail units, warehouses, and land. Simply describe the property type when you create a listing and the AI will adjust its output accordingly.
